This is a tea box redesign that I made for a fictional company called "The Pawsome Brew." The goal here was to design a cute, inviting and fun packaging while involving the tea theme; I designed the cat with a witch's hat and cauldron as a play on "brew" and made the green lettering matching the flavour, matcha.
